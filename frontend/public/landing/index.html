FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Nadil AI support Arabic dialects?

Yes! Nadil AI is built to understand Gulf, Levantine, Egyptian, and Modern Standard Arabic. It switches seamlessly between Arabic and English in the same conversation.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are live in under 15 minutes. Link your WhatsApp account, upload your menu or FAQ, and you're ready to go.

Can Nadil AI handle complex orders?

Absolutely. Nadil AI handles customizations, add-ons, special requests, and multi-item orders. If it gets stuck, it escalates to a human seamlessly.

What channels does it work with?

Nadil AI works with WhatsApp Business, website chat widget, Instagram DM, and Facebook Messenger. All in one unified dashboard.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Start with the free Starter plan — no credit card required. Upgrade to Pro when you need more conversations and advanced features.
